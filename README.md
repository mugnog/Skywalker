**Skywalker** is a professional-grade fitness intelligence platform designed for cyclists. It bridges the gap between raw data and actionable coaching. By combining **Garmin Connect** data with the **Google Gemini 2.0 AI**, Skywalker acts as a virtual coach that understands your physiology, fatigue, and life stress.

---

## üß† The Skywalker Philosophy
Skywalker isn't just a tracker; it's a training system based on the latest sports science:
* **Pyramidal Training Model:** Focus on the 70/20/10 distribution (Seiler). Nevertheless, you can decide which trainigs from you prefer.
* **Physiological Optimization:** Targeted FTP increase and VLamax reduction (Mader/Coggan).
* **Health-First Coaching:** Adjusts intensity based on Sleep Score, HRV, and subjective Stress levels.

---

## ‚ú® Key Features
* **Automated Data Sync:** Seamlessly fetches activities and health stats from Garmin.
* **Performance Matrix:** CTL (Fitness), ATL (Fatigue), and TSB (Form) tracking.
* **AI Coach "Skywalker":** Chat with an AI agent that knows your history and current recovery state.
* **Pro XML Workout Generator:** Generates `.zwo` files for Zwift including **Live Coaching Text Events** and structured intervals.
* **Wahoo & Zwift Integration:** Supports Virtual Rides (Zwift) and Outdoor sessions (Garmin/Wahoo/Coros and more) with smart cadence/power detection.
* **Daily Check-in:** Subjective feeling & strain matrix to align data with your actual "feel."
